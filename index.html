Understanding Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic condition where the body either doesn't produce enough insulin or can't use it effectively, leading to elevated blood sugar levels. Early detection and management are key to preventing serious health complications.

Risk Factors for Diabetes

Your risk for diabetes may be higher if you are overweight, have a family history of diabetes, or have high blood pressure. Other factors include age, lack of exercise, and unhealthy diet. Know your risk to take preventive action.

Healthy Lifestyle Tips
  • Manage or reduce your risk of diabetes with small, sustainable changes.
  • Incorporate balanced meals, regular exercise, and stress management into your routine.
  • Every step counts towards a healthier you.
